Challenge the tyranny of perfection.

The world is at peace.

The Final War has been fought, homelessness and poverty are a relic of the past, and the nations of the world have established rules of equality for all. For the first time, citizens of Earth are experiencing what can only be called a perfect utopia.

Tiala Hartlight is having none of that.

Help Tiala as she enters a series of ethereal realms which house the power that stablizes the world know as the Dreamscape to shatter the Dreamdrop totems to revert the world back to the chaos it once was.

Defy destiny and take back your fate. The world will be what you make it.
