Character Bio/Personality: Alulu Dike is a nightmare to face in combat. Her weapon of choice is the Light Blade Revolver, a magically transforming weapon that is capable of assuming the form of a sword, dual handguns, a scythe, a sniper rifle and a snake blade. Her most common fighting style uses the LBR as a set of a handgun and sword. The Light Blade Revolver has two modes: the crystal blue mode is ethereal and does no physical damage to the enemy, while the ruby mode is an actual weapon and can kill.
