Just as a guillotine has brought many a royal line to termination, so also does Blade Guillotine for the Lovely Hearts. A ruthless opponent, Blade seeks and destroys the Hearts in a relentless fashion, her mere appearance being enough to tip an even balance and snatch defeat from the jaws of victory.

Blade Guillotine has also been seen as an unofficial extra member of the Starlite 7th, having save the team on a number of desperate occasions. That she aids on group and hurts the other is a source of endless speculation and part of the tension between the Starlite 7th and the Lovely Hearts.

Blade Guillotine weilds an unadorned epee in combat and is a speedy attacker.
