Character Bio/Personality: Alulu is the type that gets what she wants, consequences be damned. Friends are only needed to get what you want and to be discarded if that gets you closest to the goal. She plays her cards close to her chest and deftly manipulates others for her own ends. That she has friends at all is a wonder in itself.

Alulu wields the Executionist, a black bladed sword said to be able to cut down a million foes without losing its edge. It's incredibly light weight and Alulu's ambidextrous whirling fighting style makes her a terror to face.
