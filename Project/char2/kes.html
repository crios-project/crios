Character Bio/Personality: While she is no longer part of the Starlit 7th, Kesshō is every bit the fighter the Starlit Mikado was. Having switched to a reverse gripped blade, Kesshō has graduated to a style of perfect blade work that has no equal. Her blade is Tenebris Operiet, a black diamond blade with a monofliament edge. When pressed, Kesshō switches to a dual wielding style, using her magic to give her normally blunt sheath as keen an edge as her sword.
