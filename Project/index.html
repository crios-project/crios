• A crios is a multicolored woven woolen belt. In a similar sense, the Crios project is a story that is woven with a variety of diverse characters and told through a number of different mediums including video games, tabletop games, comics, audio dramas, novels, and eventually animation. My personal goal is to be the main architect and narrative lead of the Crios project and to have masterful collaborators help to bring this vision forth. The end goal is to not only entertain, but to craft something that uses storytelling in every medium to tell a story that can only be told via that medium.

    Davis Emmanuel
    creator of the Crios Project
