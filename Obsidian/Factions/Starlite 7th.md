### Overview

The **Starlite 7th** are a covert assembly of System-selected champions tasked with defending Royastella from existential threats, anomalous phenomena, and catastrophic destabilization events. They function as an autonomous heroic force operating beyond governmental authority, political influence, or formal military command.

Their existence is largely speculative to the general public. Most sightings are dismissed as rumor, spectacle, or misidentified System phenomena. The Starlite 7th deliberately operate from obscurity, prioritizing outcome over recognition.

---
### Purpose

The collective exists to maintain equilibrium between civilization, System integrity, and emergent threats capable of overwhelming conventional defense measures.

They are not enforcers of law, nor are they representatives of any ruling body. Instead, they act as corrective agents deployed when threats exceed the capacity of ordinary societal structures.

---
### The System

The Starlite 7th derive their abilities through the **Royastellan System**, a benevolent infrastructure that distributes skills, enhancements, and combat abilities.

#### Key Properties of the System
- Grants **Starlight Blessings**, which are skill-based augmentations rather than magical spells.  
- Assigns **Runic Roles**, symbolic titles linked to combat specialization and thematic identity.  
- Selects candidates independent of human influence or social standing.  
- Operates as a stabilizing and protective global mechanism.  

The System does not grant political power, command authority, or institutional control. Its function is purely preservational.

---
### Operational Doctrine

The Starlite 7th prioritize:

- Civilian protection  
- Rapid threat neutralization  
- Minimal collateral damage  
- Information containment  
- Intermember tactical synergy  

They typically operate outside public view, engaging threats in isolated environments or altered spatial zones generated by the System.

---
### Secrecy & Public Perception

The identities of all members are completely classified. Even among elite governmental or corporate entities, concrete evidence of their existence remains elusive.

Public awareness generally manifests as:
- Urban legends  
- Viral combat footage of unclear origin  
- Conspiracy discourse regarding System avatars  
- Cultural mythologizing  

Ironically, many members maintain highly visible civilian careers, though their heroic identities remain undiscovered due to System-level concealment effects and dramatic personality divergence between identities.

---
### Combat Methodology

The Starlite 7th specialize in synchronized team combat. Their abilities are designed to interlock, creating exponential effectiveness when deployed collectively.
#### Tactical Characteristics
- Multidomain battlefield coverage  
- Blessing synergy chains  
- Distributed role specialization  
- Real-time mental coordination via [[Kayre]]’s telepathic network  
- Strategic command modeling directed by [[Daeon Liàng]]  

---
### Starlight Blessings

Each member possesses a unique System-granted skill. These Blessings operate as advanced combat abilities rather than traditional spellcasting.

Blessings:
- Cannot be learned naturally  
- Are unique to the bearer  
- Often reflect psychological or symbolic aspects of the wielder  
- Scale with experience and System synchronization  

---
### Transformation & Runic Manifestation

Members can transition into Runic Knight forms via Mana activation. These transformations:
- Manifest specialized armor and weaponry  
- Enhance physical and cognitive performance  
- Enable Blessing deployment  

Runic identities often display drastically amplified personality traits compared to civilian forms.

---
### Thematic Identity

The Starlite 7th visually and structurally reflect heroic archetypes reminiscent of mythic sentai-style defenders. Their aesthetic emphasizes:

- Color-coded individuality  
- Distinct symbolic motifs  
- Coordinated but independent combat specialization  

Their unified visual presence reinforces their function as a collective defensive constellation.

---
### Known Operational Patterns

The Starlite 7th typically appear during:

- System destabilization events  
- Manifestations of corrupted or rogue constructs  
- Dimensional interference phenomena  
- Escalating conflicts between high-tier combatants  

They rarely intervene in mundane or localized incidents.

---
### Internal Dynamics

Though united in purpose, interpersonal relationships vary widely:

- Emotional leadership often gravitates toward [[Itami Serafino]]  
- Strategic leadership is maintained by [[Daeon Liàng]]  
- Emotional stabilization and loyalty reinforcement frequently originate from [[Shini Thana]]  
- Tactical coordination is maintained through [[Kayre]]’s telepathic linking  
- Aggressive frontline disruption is typically performed by [[Riasa Sungbae]]  
- Adaptive battlefield improvisation often comes from [[Wentea Liso]]  
- Efficiency and power amplification frequently originates from [[Takino Retsu]]  

---
### Fail-Safe Protocol

If all members are incapacitated, Marate joins the battle as Starlit Alpha, retrieves the fallen Runic Knights, and activates **Heaven’s Mandate** transforming in The Golden Imperator, representing the System’s final defensive contingency.

---
### Symbolism

The Starlite 7th represent:

- Collective heroism over individual glory  
- Destiny selected by impartial cosmic infrastructure  
- Harmony between autonomy and cooperation  
- The preservation of societal continuity through unseen guardianship  

---
### Trivia (Out-of-Universe)

- The Starlite 7th are inspired by **Sentai / Power Rangers team structures**.  
- Their Runic titles intentionally evoke monarchal naming schemes to reinforce symbolic grandeur rather than political rank.  
- The group is designed narratively to embody the concept of heroic myth existing alongside modern celebrity culture.
