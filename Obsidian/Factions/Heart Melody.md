### Overview

**Heart Melody** is a collective of magically empowered heroines whose abilities derive from **harmonic resonance**, emotional synchronization, and musical metaphysical structures. Each member manifests a unique **Heart Form**, representing a distinct emotional philosophy and magical specialization.

Unlike traditional magical systems that rely on raw power or technical incantation, Heart Melody functions through **harmonic compatibility**, where strength emerges from emotional cohesion and relational bonds between members.

The team is sustained by a shared metaphysical structure known as the **Harmonic Lattice**, a living magical network that allows their Essentia, casting stability, and power scaling to function cooperatively.

---
### Magical Framework

#### Harmonic Casting

Heart Melody magic operates through layered vocalization, emotional resonance, and synchronized will. Spells manifest as structured musical expressions rather than isolated incantations.

#### Essentia

Each Heart channels a unique magical spectrum known as **Essentia**, representing both elemental affinity and philosophical alignment.

#### Tier Structure

| Tier | Name | Function |
|--------|------------|----------------|
| Tier 1 | Essentia | Individual elemental casting |
| Tier 2 | Entwinement | Multi-Heart cooperative magic |
| Tier 3 | Lúmira | Harmonic transcendence |
| Tier 4 | Ómura | Reality-distorting harmonic collapse |
| Tier 5 | Æónel | Absolute metaphysical override |

---
### The Harmonic Lattice

The Harmonic Lattice binds all Hearts into a shared magical ecosystem. It enables:

- Spell stabilization  
- Emotional energy distribution  
- Cooperative amplification  
- Harmonic recovery after magical strain  

Damage to one Heart affects the structural integrity of the entire lattice.

### Structural Catastrophe: The Loss of Heart Aria

The destruction of Heart Aria causes permanent damage to the Harmonic Lattice. This event results in:

- Loss of full casting synchronization  
- Increased strain on cooperative magic  
- Creation of an unresolved harmonic absence  

The lattice continues functioning but remains incomplete.

---
### Mahana Paradox

The entity known as Mahana Sahi originates from a **Desynchronization Event** triggered by a Tier 4 harmonic strike.

This phenomenon:

- Fractures harmonic identity  
- Relocates core melodic essence across realities  
- Forces the receiving reality to reconstruct identity parameters  

Mahana is a reconstructed harmonic echo attempting to stabilize a melody that was never meant to continue.
