**Poison** is a conjoined elemental force representing corrosive vitality, transformative decay, and weaponized biological destabilization. It embodies the paradox of life and destruction operating simultaneously, enabling magic that erodes, infects, mutates, and destabilizes targets over time. Within Lúmenai, Poison is an Entwinement expressing living corruption and alchemical entropy. In Cymora, Poison manifests through fluid, burning resonance that destabilizes both physical and metaphysical structures. Within the System of Royastella, Poison-aligned abilities exist primarily among external invaders, as conjoined elemental manifestations are canonically exclusive to hostile forces opposing the realm. Poison is corrosive vitality and burning fluidity.

## Lúmenai — Nóxira

### Classification
Tier 2 — Conjoined Element  
Vaelóri Entwinement  
(Pýrua + Áquora)

### Essentia Profile
**Nóxira** represents the simultaneous expression of ignition and adaptation. Rather than producing simple damage, Nóxira introduces living corruption that spreads, evolves, and alters the environment or target over time.

Nóxira is often associated with alchemical transformation, biological manipulation, and slow destabilization rather than immediate destructive force.

### Magical Expression

Common manifestations include:

- Toxic flame and corrosive liquid constructs  
- Progressive affliction and debilitation effects  
- Alchemical mutation and biological destabilization  
- Environmental rot and terrain contamination  
- Venomous enhancement of physical or magical attacks  

### Harmonic Behavior

Nóxira demands balance between Pýrua’s aggressive transformation and Áquora’s adaptive continuity. Excess ignition results in unstable, self-destructive corruption, while excessive fluidity reduces offensive lethality.

## Cymora — Tânahr

### Classification
Compound Elemental Root  
(Fire + Water)

### Linguistic Role
**Tânahr** produces resonance patterns that oscillate between flowing vowels and sharp ignition consonants, reflecting the unstable and invasive nature of poison magic.

The incantation rhythm often mimics spreading contamination through cascading syllabic progression.

### Sonic Properties

- Alternating smooth and harsh vocal transitions  
- Sustained vowels representing propagation and spread  
- Sudden consonant bursts representing ignition or infection triggers  
- Repetitive cadence reinforcing continuous degradation  

### Compound Cymoran Behavior

Tânahr is typically used in spells that:

- Generate corrosive or venomous constructs  
- Spread progressive magical contamination  
- Disrupt healing or restoration harmonics  
- Induce mutation or structural instability  
- Bind targets through internal corrosion rather than external restraint

## System Mana — Poison Manifestation

### Canon Classification
Enemy-Exclusive Conjoined Elemental Discipline

### Canon Note

Within Royastella, conjoined (Tier 2 equivalent) elemental expressions **do not naturally occur among native elemental cores**. Poison-based manifestations are therefore associated exclusively with hostile external forces attempting territorial expansion into Royastella.

The **Runic Knights of Starlite 7th** actively combat invading forces known to deploy biochemical and magical contamination strategies, often intended to weaken entire ecosystems or populations prior to conquest.

### Functional Nature

Poison manifestations in System combat function as biological warfare disciplines designed to destabilize enemies through attrition rather than direct confrontation.

### Mechanical Properties

- Damage-over-time affliction techniques  
- Mana-corrupting toxins that disrupt spellcasting  
- Area contamination fields  
- Biological mutation and infection abilities  
- Environmental degradation and ecosystem collapse techniques  

### Tactical Doctrine

Enemy Poison specialists often serve as:

- Battlefield destabilizers  
- Attrition warfare operatives  
- Anti-healer and anti-support combatants  
- Contamination scouts preparing territories for invasion

Across Crios cosmology, Poison commonly symbolizes:

- Transformation through decay  
- Corruption as evolutionary force  
- The inevitability of internal collapse
