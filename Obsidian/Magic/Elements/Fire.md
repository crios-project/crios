**Fire** is a primary elemental force appearing across all three major magical frameworks within the Crios cosmology: **Lúmenai (Beeston)**, **Cymora (Nexsicon)**, and **System Mana (Royastella)**. While each system recognizes Fire as a foundational energetic principle, its metaphysical interpretation, method of invocation, and functional expression vary significantly between realms. Fire is consuming heat, passion, and ignition. 

---

## Overview

| Magic System | Element Name | Nature of Expression | Governing Principle |
|-------------|-------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Lúmenai | **Pýrua** | Emotional harmonic resonance | Passion as ignition |
| Cymora | **Tânor** | Sonic incantational command | Spoken resonance |
| System Mana | Fire Core | Internalized energy engineering | Stored energetic output |

---

## Lúmenai Interpretation

### Pýrua

**Tier:** Tier 1 — Basic Elements  
**System:** Lúmenai  
**Realm:** Beeston  

#### Essence
Pýrua represents ignition through emotional intensity. Unlike physical combustion, Pýrua manifests when will, intent, and passion align into harmonic stability.

Fire in Lúmenai is considered one of the most volatile Essentia, as emotional imbalance directly destabilizes its resonance.

#### Functional Characteristics

- Emotional amplification
- Catalytic transformation
- Rapid energetic escalation
- Harmonic destabilization potential

Pýrua frequently acts as a catalyst Essentia, serving as a required component for multiple Vaelóri entwinements.

#### Vaelóri Entwinements Derived from Pýrua

| Entwinement | Essentia Name | Additional Element |
|-------------|--------------|--------------------|
| Poison | Nóxira | Water (Áquora) |
| Metal | Férroma | Earth (Téroma) |
| Lightning | Sýlphae | Wind (Vaélia) |

#### Harmonic Behavior

Pýrua responds strongly to emotional sincerity. Artificial emotional projection weakens resonance output and increases harmonic fracture risk.

## Cymora Interpretation

### Tânor

**Pronunciation:** TÂ-nor  
**System:** Cymora  
**Realm:** Nexsicon  

#### Magical Nature

Tânor is not generated internally but invoked through precise sonic resonance. Cymora treats Fire as a performative word, where phonetic structure directly reshapes reality.

The effectiveness of Tânor depends on:

- Rhythm
- Cadence
- Vowel elongation
- Incantational stability

#### Elemental Compositions Involving Tânor

| Compound Element | Cymoran Name | Combination |
|------------------|--------------|------------|
| Poison | Tânahr | Fire + Water |
| Lightning | Tângwynt | Fire + Wind |
| Metal | Tândâr | Fire + Earth |

#### Ritual Behavior

Tânor often appears within four-line incantational structures. Unlike Lúmenai, emotional alignment enhances resonance but is not required for activation.

---

## System Mana Interpretation

### Fire Elemental Core

**System:** SYSTEM Mana  
**Realm:** Royastella  

#### Magical Nature

Fire within the System is treated as measurable energetic output stored internally as mana. Fire abilities are engineered through trained spell techniques rather than expressive harmonic or sonic processes.

#### Core Characteristics

- Stored energetic reserves
- Technique-based activation
- Efficiency optimization
- Capacity-based limitations

System Fire is typically stable and predictable, with performance governed by training, refinement, and mana volume.

---

## External Core Manifestation

Certain Royastellan practitioners can manifest their elemental core as a physical aura. This phenomenon was formalized among the **Runic Knights of Starlite 7th** after instruction by dissonant discord.

Manifested Fire auras can produce:

- Enhanced physical acceleration
- Increased strike force
- Heightened combat endurance
- Cognitive sharpening

## Cross-System Interactions

When Fire manifestations from different systems interact, outcomes vary depending on dominance hierarchy:

- Lúmenai Fire tends to override System Fire when emotional resonance exceeds engineered output.
- Cymora Fire can disrupt both systems if incantational cadence destabilizes harmonic or mana-based structures.
- System Fire can outlast both systems in sustained engagements due to resource quantification.
