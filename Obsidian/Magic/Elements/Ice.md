**Ice** is a conjoined elemental force representing arrested motion, preservation, crystalline silence, and temporal stabilization. Ice magic governs stillness, containment, structural suspension, and controlled entropy. Within Lúmenai, Ice manifests as harmonic stasis and preservation. In Cymora, Ice emerges through resonant stillness and freezing cadence. Within the System of Royastella, Ice-aligned abilities exist primarily among external invaders, as conjoined elemental manifestations are canonically exclusive to hostile forces targeting the realm. Ice is arrested motion and crystalline silence.

## Lúmenai — Crýalune

### Classification
Tier 2 — Conjoined Element  
Vaelóri Entwinement  
(Áquora + Vaélia)

### Essentia Profile
**Crýalune** represents the simultaneous voicing of flow and motion brought into suspended equilibrium. Rather than simple cold, Crýalune creates magical states where movement, decay, and transformation are halted or slowed to near permanence.

Crýalune is strongly associated with preservation, containment, memory stabilization, and temporal suspension.

### Magical Expression

Common manifestations include:

- Ice construct formation and crystalline barriers  
- Temporal slowing fields  
- Stasis binding and immobilization magic  
- Preservation of biological or magical states  
- Terrain restructuring through crystalline growth  

### Harmonic Behavior

Crýalune demands careful resonance balance between Áquora’s adaptive memory and Vaélia’s directional force. Excess fluidity produces fragile or melting constructs, while excessive motion collapses the stasis field into brittle instability.

## Cymora — Gwynahr

### Classification
Compound Elemental Root  
(Wind + Water)

### Linguistic Role
**Gwynahr** produces smooth, flowing vocalization interrupted by crisp consonant stops, imitating the natural crystallization process. The resonance pattern emphasizes containment, layering, and harmonic stillness.

### Sonic Properties

- Extended vowels to simulate slowing temporal flow  
- Soft consonant transitions to represent freezing propagation  
- Repetitive cadence reinforcing structural layering  
- Controlled breath spacing to sustain containment harmonics  

### Compound Cymoran Behavior

Gwynahr is typically used in spells that:

- Create crystalline or frozen barriers  
- Suspend movement or spellcasting  
- Preserve magical constructs or living forms  
- Restrain or immobilize targets  
- Establish environmental control zones

## System Mana — Ice Manifestation

### Canon Classification
Enemy-Exclusive Conjoined Elemental Discipline

### Canon Note

Within Royastella, conjoined (Tier 2 equivalent) elemental expressions **do not naturally occur among native elemental cores**. Ice-based manifestations are therefore associated exclusively with external invasion forces attempting to destabilize Royastella’s ecosystems and infrastructure.

The **Runic Knights of Starlite 7th** actively defend against such incursions, frequently encountering battlefield control specialists utilizing large-scale freezing or containment tactics.

### Functional Nature

System Ice manifestations focus on battlefield denial, immobilization, and strategic environmental manipulation rather than raw destructive output.

### Mechanical Properties

- Area immobilization fields  
- Structural reinforcement and defensive constructs  
- Movement suppression techniques  
- Environmental freezing and terrain control  
- Mana flow dampening through containment matrices  

### Tactical Doctrine

Enemy Ice specialists typically function as:

- Battlefield controllers  
- Defensive bulwark units  
- Territory stabilization agents for invading forces  
- Crowd and army immobilization specialists
