**Light** is a supremal elemental domain representing restorative truth, harmonic correction, and existential revelation. Unlike foundational or conjoined elements, Light operates above elemental interaction, overriding lower-tier magical expressions through resonance authority rather than elemental opposition. Within the Crios cosmology, Light is classified as a High Harmonic Domain and is universally rare, costly, and structurally dominant over Tier 1 and Tier 2 magic systems. Light is restorative resonance and truth made audible. Healing is not “repair” but re-harmonization. 

---

## Canon Rarity and Casting Risk

Light is not naturally accessible to most casters across any realm.

### Elemental Core Availability

| Core Type | Natural Occurrence |
|----------|-------------|
| Light Elemental Core | Extremely rare |
| Dark Elemental Core | Even rarer |
| Aether Elemental Core | Impossible |

### Failure Consequences

- Light spell failure results in catastrophic energetic backlash.
- Most common consequence is total magical exhaustion and unconsciousness.
- Severe failures may cause long-term harmonic instability or permanent magical burnout.

### Power Hierarchy

Light spells inherently override:

- All Tier 1 Elements  
- All Tier 2 Entwinements  

Light is subordinate only to:

- Dark Supremacy  
- Aether Supremacy

## Lúmenai — Lúmira

### Classification
Tier 3 — Supremacy Essentia

### Essentia Profile
**Lúmira** represents truth made audible and restoration through harmonic realignment. Healing within Lúmenai is not cellular repair but re-synchronization of a subject’s existence with its intended harmonic state.

Lúmira effects frequently appear radiant, refractive, or prismatically resonant, symbolizing the restoration of internal order.

### Magical Expression

Common manifestations include:

- Harmonic healing and restoration  
- Purification of corrupted Essentia  
- Revelation of hidden truths or illusions  
- Reinforcement of oaths and binding contracts  
- Stabilization of volatile magical systems  
- Resurrection-adjacent stabilization (extremely rare and incomplete)  

### Harmonic Behavior

Lúmira requires:

- Absolute emotional clarity  
- Resonant authority  
- Prolonged harmonic chant structures  

Incomplete or emotionally conflicted casting can cause resonance collapse and backlash.

## Cymora — Alor

### Classification
Primary Supremal Elemental Root

### Linguistic Role
**Alor** is a high-resonance Cymoran root representing illumination, revelation, and restorative harmonic force. Cymoran Light incantations rely heavily on tonal purity, sustained vowel elongation, and symmetrical rhythmic patterns.

### Sonic Properties

- Long, sustained vowel resonance to maintain harmonic stability  
- Balanced cadence emphasizing structural symmetry  
- Clear tonal articulation required to prevent harmonic fracture  

### Cymoran Light Behavior

Alor is typically invoked to:

- Purify corruption or hostile magical influence  
- Reveal concealed or altered realities  
- Restore damaged magical constructs  
- Strengthen protective wards or sacred bindings  
- Stabilize collapsing harmonic fields  

Cymoran Light casting is extremely demanding vocally and mentally. Minor phonetic errors can collapse spell integrity.

## System Mana — Light Manifestation

### Canon Classification
Exceptional Core-Type Manifestation  
Non-Standard System Functionality

### Functional Nature

Within Royastella, Light is not a conventional elemental output of the System. It is associated exclusively with anomalous or ultra-rare elemental cores. Light magic is incompatible with standard mana efficiency models and consumes exponentially higher internal resources.

### Mechanical Properties

- Absolute purification effects  
- High-grade restoration beyond conventional System healing  
- Reality-stabilization events  
- Aura-based reinforcement and empowerment  
- Disruption or erasure of hostile magical constructs  

### Operational Limitations

- Extreme mana consumption  
- Severe neurological and physiological fatigue  
- Risk of complete System collapse within the caster  
- Potential long-term damage to elemental core stability
