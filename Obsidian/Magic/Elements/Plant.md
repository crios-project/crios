**Plant** is a conjoined elemental force representing growth, memory embedded within living matter, regeneration, and environmental symbiosis. Plant governs biomancy, terrain shaping, life acceleration, and organic adaptation. Within Lúmenai, Plant manifests as harmonic life resonance between memory and structure. In Cymora, Plant appears through layered, growth-pattern incantations designed to awaken latent vitality. Within the System of Royastella, Plant-aligned manifestations are classified as invasive or hostile external entwinements and are associated with expansionist or parasitic magical threats. Plant is living structure and growth with memory.

## Lúmenai — Víratha

### Classification
Tier 2 — Conjoined Element  
Vaelóri Entwinement  
(Áquora + Téroma)

### Essentia Profile
**Víratha** represents life structured through memory. It is the Essentia of growth that remembers its origins, allowing magic to shape living matter with continuity and preservation. Víratha practitioners often display strong emotional ties to environment, community, and long-term stability.

Víratha does not create life from nothing; it accelerates, reshapes, or harmonizes existing organic systems.

### Magical Expression

Common manifestations include:

- Plant growth acceleration  
- Biological regeneration and healing support  
- Terrain manipulation through root and vine control  
- Organic barrier formation  
- Seed memory activation and life restoration  
- Symbiotic plant constructs  

### Harmonic Behavior

Víratha requires emotional continuity and environmental awareness. Practitioners who lose focus or emotional grounding may cause uncontrolled growth, parasitic expansion, or unstable organic constructs.

## Cymora — Nahdâr

### Classification
Compound Elemental Root  
(Water + Earth)

### Linguistic Role
**Nahdâr** uses layered tonal repetition designed to mimic natural growth cycles. Cymoran Plant incantations frequently rely on slow cadence, vowel elongation, and rhythm patterns mirroring root spread or vine expansion.

### Sonic Properties

- Sustained vowel resonance reflecting gradual growth  
- Layered incantation phrasing representing organic branching  
- Stable consonant anchoring reinforcing environmental binding  
- Multi-line rhythmic repetition simulating natural life cycles  

### Compound Cymoran Behavior

Nahdâr is commonly used in spells that:

- Accelerate plant growth and environmental restoration  
- Summon or shape living vegetation constructs  
- Enhance regenerative biological processes  
- Bind or immobilize targets using organic matter  
- Manipulate ecosystems or natural terrain  
- Establish protective living barriers

## System Mana — External Plant Entwinement

### Canon Classification
External Hostile Element  
Enemy Expansion-Class Manifestation

### Functional Nature

In Royastella, Plant entwinements are not naturally compatible with native elemental cores. Canon doctrine classifies all Tier 2 entwinements, including Plant, as invasive forces primarily wielded by foreign entities or interrealm aggressors.

Plant-based threats often weaponize ecological takeover, biological assimilation, or environmental domination.

### Mechanical Properties

- Rapid terrain conversion through invasive growth  
- Biological absorption or parasitic assimilation  
- Regenerative enemy fortification  
- Living siege constructs  
- Battlefield obstruction through organic expansion  
- Environmental corruption or ecosystem hijacking  

### Tactical Doctrine

Plant-based invaders frequently operate as:

- Territory denial forces  
- Long-duration siege or occupation specialists  
- Environmental destabilization agents  
- Regenerative frontline or defensive reinforcement entities  

Starlite 7th doctrine identifies Plant entwinement outbreaks as high-risk expansion scenarios requiring containment or eradication.
