### Royastellan System-Regulated Mana Magic
*Crios Project Canon*

---
## Overview

Within the metaphysical framework of Royastella, mana is neither ambient nor universally distributed. Instead, it is intrinsically bound to the Element Core System, a biological–spiritual organ construct that defines an individual’s magical capacity, affinity, and metaphysical identity. Royastellan mana therefore functions as a core-generated resonance energy, not a free-flowing environmental resource.

---
### Fundamental Principles

Royastellan mana originates from an Element Core, a crystallized metaphysical node located within the astral-physical overlap of a being’s soul architecture. The core converts existential vitality into elemental resonance, which manifests as spellcasting, elemental manipulation, and enhanced physiological phenomena.  

Unlike conventional mana models that treat magical energy as external or universally accessible, Royastellan theory asserts:

- Mana is identity-bound
- Mana is elementally restricted
- Mana expression is governed by resonance compatibility
- Mana output scales with core stability and harmonic synchronization

---
## Element Core Classifications
### Native Royastellan Cores

Native cores represent the primordial elemental alignments believed to originate from Royastella’s planetary formation mythos. These elements are stable, widely occurring, and form the baseline for Royastellan magical doctrine.

Primary Native Elements

- Fire
- Water
- Earth
- Wind

Individuals born with Native cores generally demonstrate:

- Higher stability
- Predictable spell development pathways
- Strong cultural integration within Royastellan magical academies

---

### Non-Native Royastellan Cores

Non-Native cores emerge from hybridization or inter-realm resonance contamination. Any hybrid cores that exist come from invaders. 

Composite Elements

- Ice    
- Poison
- Lightning
- Metal 
- Plant
- Martial


Non-Native cores typically exhibit:

- Greater raw variability
- Higher resonance volatility
- Expanded ability combinations
- Increased risk of harmonic destabilization

---

### High Tier Manifestations

#### Light Core

Represents harmonic amplification, purification, and ontological reinforcement. Light cores possess strong defensive, restorative, and anti-entropy properties.
#### Dark Core

Associated with entropy manipulation, shadow resonance, and dimensional distortion. Dark cores are often stigmatized due to their capacity to destabilize narrative probability and existential continuity.

High Tier cores manifest extremely rarely and often indicate individuals with cross-realm significance.

---
### Impossible Core States

#### Supremacy Cores

- Neon Ultra classification    
- Metaphysically impossible
- Represents total elemental dominion beyond Royastellan cosmological balance
#### Unaspected Cores

- Neutral or elementless cores
- Theoretically incompatible with Royastellan mana laws
- No verified existence in recorded history

## Core Immutability Doctrine
  
For most of Royastellan history, Element Cores were believed to be permanently fixed at manifestation. This belief formed the basis of Royastellan magical social hierarchy, educational structure, and combat doctrine.

The doctrine remained unchallenged until the intervention of [[dissonant discord]], whose teachings revealed that:

- Element Cores can undergo Resonant Recomposition
- Core element signatures can be rewritten through extreme harmonic exposure
- Physical manifestation of elemental identity dramatically enhances mana output

---

## Physical Manifestation Phenomenon

  

Under advanced resonance alignment, users may achieve Elemental Embodiment, a state in which:

- The user’s physiology partially transforms into their elemental nature
    
- Mana efficiency increases exponentially
    
- Spellcasting latency approaches zero
    
- Core energy begins interacting directly with environmental metaphysics
    

  

This technique was first successfully demonstrated to Starlite 7th through Discord’s instruction, marking a paradigm shift in Royastellan magical theory.

---

## Cymoran Quantification Integration

  

Royastellan mana, while traditionally experiential and intuitive, later became quantifiable through Cymoran Structure Analysis. This system allows researchers and elite magi to measure:

- Lúmenail Harmonics (core resonance frequency)
    
- Output efficiency ratios
    
- Entropy leakage rates
    
- Cross-element compatibility thresholds
    

  

This integration became critical during the Realm Convergence Era, allowing Royastellan scholars to model mana interactions across merged realities.
