# Realm Convergence

## Overview

**Realm Convergence** is the large-scale cosmological phenomenon in which two or more independent realities begin gravitationally, narratively, and metaphysically merging into a single shared existence. Unlike standard interdimensional travel or temporary planar overlap, Realm Convergence represents a permanent integration of ontological frameworks.

During convergence events:

- Physical laws begin blending
- Magic systems overlap or hybridize
- Cultural and historical timelines collide
- Geographic territories fuse or overwrite each other
- Mythological or dormant realms may rematerialize
- Identity continuity across realities becomes unstable

Realm Convergence is considered one of the most dangerous and transformative events known within Crios cosmology.

---

## Foundational Mechanism

Realm Convergence is driven primarily by **Resonance Gravitation**, a phenomenon where realities possessing compatible metaphysical frequencies begin to attract one another.

The most commonly accepted catalyst for convergence is the presence or relocation of a **[[Lodesoul]]**.

### Lodesoul Interaction

According to the [[Lodesoul Theorem]]:

- Lodesouls function as reality-anchor resonance nodes
- Relocating a Lodesoul causes their originating realm to follow their harmonic signature
- Multiple Lodesoul relocations accelerate multi-realm merging

This process does not occur instantly. Instead, convergence unfolds through escalating phases of instability.

---

## Stages of Realm Convergence

### Stage I — Resonance Detection

Early warning indicators appear as minor metaphysical anomalies:

- Probability fluctuations
- Magical interference patterns
- Memory inconsistencies among sensitive individuals
- Spontaneous cross-realm visions or dreams

These anomalies are often dismissed as localized magical disturbances.

---

### Stage II — Dimensional Bleed

Reality boundaries begin weakening, allowing limited cross-realm intrusion.

Common manifestations include:

- Foreign ecosystems appearing temporarily
- Entities slipping between realities
- Partial geographic overlays
- Sudden manifestation of lost or mythic locations

Magical interference becomes significantly more volatile during this stage.

---

### Stage III — Structural Overlap

Physical and metaphysical infrastructure begins merging.

Observed consequences include:

- Permanent geographic fusion
- Cultural and historical timeline contradictions
- Magic system entanglement
- Emergence of hybrid magical phenomena

At this stage, reversing convergence becomes nearly impossible.

---

### Stage IV — Ontological Integration

Reality frameworks rewrite themselves into a unified continuity.

Results include:

- Formation of a singular stabilized realm
- Permanent coexistence of merged physical laws
- Reconstruction of shared historical memory
- Extinction or assimilation of incompatible existential constructs

This stage represents the final stabilization of the convergence event.

## Magic System Interactions

### Cymoran Integration

During convergence, Cymoran magic demonstrates high adaptability due to its linguistic and structural nature. Cymoran spell architecture can often translate or stabilize foreign magical frequencies.

---

### Royastellan Integration

Royastellan Elemental Cores function as harmonic regulators capable of anchoring destabilized magical fields. These cores are frequently used to prevent catastrophic magical collapse during early convergence stages.

---

### Emergent Hybridization

Prolonged convergence produces new magic classifications that do not belong to any single originating realm. These hybrid disciplines often demonstrate unpredictable behavior and unstable power scaling.

---

## Relationship to Cascade Resonance

The most catastrophic recorded convergence event was triggered by [[Cascade Resonance]], an unassisted Grand Magic conducted by [[Tiala Hartlight]]. This attempt accelerated convergence beyond safe stabilization thresholds, producing widespread narrative and metaphysical failure across multiple realms.

Cascade Resonance caused:

- Rapid dimensional bleed across numerous realities
- Cross-realm entity displacement
- Probability structure destabilization
- Irreversible timeline entanglement

The event remains the largest uncontrolled convergence attempt in recorded history.
