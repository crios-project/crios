### Overview

Marate Jalo stands as the mentor, failsafe, and final safeguard of [[Starlite 7th]]. Known as the **Gold Imperator**, he embodies accumulated mastery, moral authority, and overwhelming combat supremacy.

Unlike the other Runic Knights, Marate exists in a permanent state of Mana Surge, granting him constant operational readiness and unparalleled power stability.

Despite his immense strength, Marate almost never engages in combat. His role is preservation, guidance, and intervention only during catastrophic scenarios where the defeat of [[Starlite 7th]] is imminent.

---

### Civilian Identity

Marate is widely beloved within his community for his warmth, accessibility, and magnetic personality. His charisma is effortless rather than cultivated, drawing trust from strangers and loyalty from allies.

#### Behavioral Traits
- Deep emotional intelligence  
- Natural leadership presence  
- Relentless optimism toward human potential  
- Strong protective instinct  
- Philosophical worldview shaped by global awareness  

Marate personally oversees and raises a collective household of orphans. He treats each child as family, investing heavily in their emotional and educational development.

He maintains extensive awareness of global power movements and factional maneuvering. This awareness is not born from paranoia, but from a deeply rooted belief that stability must be quietly maintained.

---

### Personal Philosophy

Marate believes conflict should be:
- Honest  
- Balanced  
- Worthy of both participants  

He routinely handicaps himself during combat to preserve fairness. Most of his scars are the result of deliberately limiting his own advantages.

To Marate, victory gained through overwhelming imbalance is hollow.

---

### Runic Identity

**Starlit Alpha** functions as the apex authority among Runic Knights. His presence symbolizes the System’s ultimate contingency against existential collapse.

Marate acts as:
- Instructor  
- Moral anchor  
- Emergency battlefield equalizer  
- Keeper of institutional memory  

---

### Weapon — Royal Greaves

A pair of enhanced shin guards that serve as both weapon and mobility amplifier.

#### Capabilities
- Massive kinetic force amplification  
- Extreme acceleration boosts  
- Vertical and horizontal mobility expansion  
- Devastating close-range kick-based combat  

Marate integrates Royal Greaves into a combat style centered around devastating lower-body striking techniques and aerial engagement.

---

### Heaven's Mandate

**Activation Condition:**  
Triggered when all other Runic Knights have fallen or been rendered combat ineffective.

**Effect:**  
Royal Greaves transform into a full-body combat system incorporating all Starlit Weapons.

#### Operational Enhancements
- Multi-weapon integration  
- Maximum mobility augmentation  
- System-level combat synchronization  
- Absolute battlefield dominance  

Heaven’s Mandate is considered the final defensive protocol of the Runic System. This shift also engenders the transition from Starlit Alpha into [[The Gold Imperator]]. 

---

### Starlight Blessing — Universal Blessing Access

Marate possesses unrestricted access to every Starlight Blessing utilized by other Runic Knights.

#### Functional Outcomes
- Tactical adaptability across all engagement types  
- Ability to mirror or counter allied or enemy Blessing usage  
- Seamless switching between combat disciplines  

As with all Starlight Blessings, this ability is a System-granted skill rather than traditional magic. The Royastellan System is a benevolent, stabilizing infrastructure.

---

### Combat Style

Marate combines mastery across every weapon category with devastating hand-to-hand combat.

#### Preferred Combat Tendencies
- Close-range engagement  
- Physical dominance through strikes and grappling  
- Tactical restraint to preserve opponent dignity  
- Explosive escalation when civilian safety is threatened  

He often favors:
- Brass knuckles  
- Barehanded combat  
- Weapon improvisation

### Symbolism & Thematic Role

Marate represents:
- Authority tempered by compassion  
- Strength restrained by ethics  
- Mentorship as legacy  
- The inevitability of final judgment when balance collapses  

Within the narrative structure, the Gold Imperator functions as the embodiment of last-resort justice and generational continuity.

### Etymology

- **Marate:** Slovenian for *hate*, symbolizing the adversities he absorbs and transforms.  
- **Jalo:** Finnish for *noble*, reflecting the moral and philosophical height he maintains.
