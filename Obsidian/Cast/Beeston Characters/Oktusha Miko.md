**Oktusha Miko** is a core member of the magical girl team **[[Heart Melody]]**, transforming into **[[Heart Rhythm]]**. Oktusha functions as the team’s stabilizer and tempo-keeper, anchoring Lúmenai manifestations through cadence, repetition, and physical timing via the [[Martial Arts]] entwinement of **Kórelis**.

## History

### Bio
Oktusha serves as Heart Melody’s physical and temporal anchor, ensuring that the team’s magic remains synchronized during combat and emotional strain.

### Backstory / Origin
Raised in a culture that prizes leadership and action, Oktusha internalized movement as a form of responsibility rather than expression.

### Formative Events
Her earliest successful transformation into Heart Rhythm coincided with the team’s first fully synchronized Lúmenai manifestation.

### Family / Lineage
Sister to **Omba Miko**, whose contrasting temperament complements Oktusha’s kinetic focus.

### Cultural Background
Her name and lineage draw from Choctaw linguistic roots, emphasizing leadership and environmental rhythm.

### Education / Training
Informal combat training; extensive physical conditioning through repeated field engagements.

### Past Affiliations
None outside Heart Melody.

### Secrets / Hidden History
None known.

### Reputation
Viewed as dependable and tireless; rarely credited despite her critical role.
